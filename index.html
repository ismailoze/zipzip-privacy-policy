Sign-In, Purchases and Ads

Signing in with Google Play Games is optional and only used to save your score, achievements, and character to the cloud and to show leaderboards. If you decline or skip sign-in, the Application works normally using data stored on your device.

In-app purchases (removing ads, unlocking characters) are one-time, non-consumable purchases handled entirely by Google Play Billing. The Service Provider does not receive or store your payment details.

Advertisements are served through Google AdMob. Ads shown to players who are children are not personalized and are served using child-directed, non-behavioral ad settings, in line with Google Play's Families policy.
